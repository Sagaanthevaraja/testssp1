Frequently Asked Questions

1: How does Nourish work?

Nourish connects surplus food donors with individuals and families facing food insecurity. Donors upload details of their excess inventory, volunteers offer their time for distribution, and recipients register to access available food donations.

2: Who can donate food on Nourish?

Anyone can donate food on Nourish, including restaurants, cafes, supermarkets, and food manufacturers. If you have surplus food that you'd like to share, simply upload the details to our platform.

3: How can I volunteer with Nourish?

Volunteering with Nourish is easy! Simply sign up on our platform, explore available opportunities such as food distribution and transportation, and confirm your assignments. Your time and support are invaluable in helping us combat food insecurity.

4: Who can receive food donations from Nourish?

Individuals and families facing food insecurity can receive food donations from Nourish. If you or someone you know is in need, register on our platform to access available donations tailored to your needs and preferences.

About

At Nourish, we're on a mission to combat food insecurity and reduce food waste by connecting surplus food donors with individuals and families in need. Founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to nutritious food, our platform serves as a catalyst for positive change in communities. With the support of volunteers and the dedication of our team, we're working towards a future where hunger is eradicated and every plate is filled with nourishment.
